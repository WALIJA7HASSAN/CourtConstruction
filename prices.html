How much does a new tennis court cost?

Every court is different, depending on the earthworks required, positioning and type of court surface required. Having a solid foundation is the start to achieving the best results. The addition of regular maintenance for your court, ensures it will play better and last longer.

 

New Synthetic Tennis Court

Our turnkey courts are fully inclusive of:

  • Earthworks
  • Road Base
  • 19mm Synthetic Turf
  • 4 x LED Spotlights atop 7.2m poles
  • 3 metre high black PVC mesh fence
  • Synthetic Turf Sand
  • Removable net posts with internal winder and net

Priced from $77,850* + GST

 

How long does it take to build a new court?

Our professional and highly experienced team can build your new court, from start to finish, in approx. 2 weeks (weather permitting).

* Price is based on standard earthworks and court size of 32m x 16.5m. Long distance freight, travel and accommodation not included.

 

Upgrade to Acrylic Surface Court

Any hard court surface, whether it be concrete, asphalt or bitumen, can be upgraded to a hard wearing and dynamic acrylic playing surface.

Upgrade options may include any of the following:

  • Earthworks (for new courts)
  • Concrete or asphalt foundation (for new courts)
  • Repair and level existing court surface
  • Acrylic Surface over your existing concrete, bitumen or asphalt court
  • 4 x energy-efficient 500/600w LED lights wired to base of each 8m black RHS pole
  • 3-3.6 metre high black PVC mesh fence
  • Removable net posts with internal winder and net

 

Price varies depending on requirements.
